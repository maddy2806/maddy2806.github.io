# Improving COMP110 with Pre-Lecture Videos

## Summary
I analyzed COMP110 survey data to determine whether optional pre-lecture videos would improve student learning. My idea is that short videos before lectures could help students better understand difficult concepts.

## Analysis
I used survey data including pre-lecture video support, course difficulty, pace, understanding, and prior experience. I created graphs to explore relationships between these variables.

## Conclusion
The results suggest that many students support pre-lecture videos, especially those who find the course more difficult or fast-paced. However, this data shows opinions rather than direct performance outcomes.

A good improvement would be to create short, optional videos focused on challenging topics.
